Welcome to HEXIEFOX, so glad you could join us. Little furry fox friends have all come together to teach you a bit about hexadecimal colors. Let's get our color on!

When you think of hexadecimal values think #RRGGBB (#RedGreenBlue). Hexadecimal values use numbers and letters to specify a color. 00 through FF are used to define the intensity of each color represented by RGB. 00 is the lowest and FF is the highest.

Therefore #000000 would have no color (black), and #FFFFFF would have the maximum amount of colors and result in white. #FF0000 is be red, #00FF00 is green, and #0000FF is blue.
